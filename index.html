About Didi

A seasoned Commercial, Contracts and Procurement Specialist with a passion for delivering tangible value.

With over two decades of experience as a Professional Interim, I have a proven track record of leading high-value, complex IT outsourcing, transition, and transformation programmes across the UK, Europe, and the USA. My expertise spans both public and private sector organisations, including significant engagements with central government departments, major corporations, and SMEs.

My "Why": I amd driven by the challenge of untangling complex commercial landscapes and creating order. Whether that is, for example, a Procurement Strategy, Contract Management Plans or Exit Strategies. Everything revolves around delivering tangible, lasting value for organisations. Seeing a well-executed strategy translate into significant cost savings, improved efficiency, or successful programme delivery is what motivates me.

Unique Approach/Philosophy: My approach is delivered through OPERATETM which is a strategy that I use for all clients. From initial engagement, delivery and off boarding, OPERATE TM provides a structure that combines the need to maximise as well as safe guard the client's interests with a collaborative, stakeholder-centric methodology. I believe in ensuring buy-in and successful implementation from start to finish by working closely with all parties involved.

Key Traits: My professional approach is rooted in a core belief: genuine understanding and strong relationships are fundamental to delivering exceptional results. I truly enjoy connecting with people to uncover their 'why,' as this insight is pivotal for crafting win-win solutions and fostering robust collaboration. This people-centric foundation, coupled with the flexibility and adaptability honed over 20 years of navigating diverse contracts and landscapes, empowers me to tackle complex challenges effectively. I apply a strategic, methodical mindset often leveraging my OPERATE™ framework so as to consistently deliver tangible value while safeguarding client interests.
