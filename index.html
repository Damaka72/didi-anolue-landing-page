My Expertise

Here's how I can help your organisation thrive.

Commercial & Contract Management

I manage your high-value, complex outsourcing contracts from start to finish. This means careful drafting, spotting risks early, and skilled negotiation to get you the best terms and real cost savings.

Transformation & Programme Leadership

When you're facing big IT changes or transformations, I lead the commercial, contract, and procurement side of things. I'll set up clear governance, keep all your stakeholders aligned, and make sure everything runs smoothly from a commercial and procurement perspective.

IT Category Expertise

I have in-depth knowledge across all key IT areas – Software (SaaS), Hardware, Cloud (like AWS), Telecoms, and Professional Services. This means I can negotiate effectively for you with major vendors such as Oracle, Workday, and IBM.

Public Sector Procurement

I create and put in place smart procurement strategies specifically for the public sector. This involves detailed market research, working with your teams to define exactly what’s needed, and expertly using frameworks like CCS and G Cloud to ensure you get the best value while staying compliant.

SME & Start-Up Support

For start-ups and SMEs, I design practical Procurement and Contract Management systems from the ground up, giving you a strong commercial foundation to grow sustainably.

Mentoring & Training

I help develop procurement professionals through dedicated mentoring. I also design and run practical Contract Management workshops that boost project teams’ commercial understanding and help everyone stick to the agreements.
